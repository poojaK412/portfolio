Introduction

In the realm of essential human needs, few phenomena challenge our understanding of the nocturnal landscape like sleepwalking. This UX research report embarks on a journey that goes beyond mere exploration, delving into the creation of an innovative health app tailored for sleepwalkers.

The app is poised to offer a ground-breaking solution, enabling users to monitor their sleeping patterns and behaviours, transforming data into actionable insights. This innovative app stands as a beacon of hope for sleepwalkers, as it promises to send timely alerts to caregivers and loved ones when a user embarks on a sleepwalking episode. By bridging the gap between sleepwalkers and their caregivers, this app not only ensures safety but also promotes a profound sense of reassurance and well-being.

Results and Findings

Participants reported a consistent and uninterrupted sleep experience​

Participants maintain an average sleep duration of​ 7 hours​

Participants stated “stress” to be a major cause for sleepwalking​

Participants are concerned about their family’s safety​

Problem Statement

An adult who sleepwalks needs to identify an effective method for monitoring his sleepwalking episodes because he is worried about the potential risks to both himself and those in his vicinity​
